Services

Metal Fabrication Services


We offer precision metal cutting, punching, and forming services using heavy-duty maechanical power press machines. Specialized in producing customized metal strips and components for construction, industrial hardware and door-window fabrication.

Metal Cutting Services


We provide precise metal cutting services using heavy-duty chop saw machines. Our facility can out MS/SS flats, rods, angles, and pipes in customized sizes for industrial, fabrication, and construction use. Fast and accurate cutting ensures high-quality fabrication.

Grinding & Finishing Services


We offer precise grinding, tool sharpening, and finishing services using heavy-duty bench grinders. From burr removal to tool edge shaping, we ensure high-accuracy surface prep for welding, fabrication and industrial use.

Welding & Fabrication Services


We provide reliable arc welding services for metal joining, repair, and fabrication work. Our skilled technicians use advanced JKARC machines to ensure strong and precise welding for gates, grills, machinary parts, and structural components.

Custom Welding & Fabrication Solutions


We offer tailored welding services including design samples, joint testing, and custom fabrication work. Whether it's plates, brakcets, or structural joints, we ensure strong, clean welds suitable for construction, machinary, and custom metalwork. Quality assurance and client-specific designs are our priority.
